About

Research project

MobileNetV2 Transfer Learning for Garbage Classification in Automated Waste Segregation

An undergraduate research project applying transfer learning to classify waste into six material categories. This app runs the actual trained models on-device.

How It Works
When you upload or capture an image, it is resized to 224×224 and passed through the actual trained model (converted to TensorFlow.js). The model outputs a probability for each of the six categories, and the highest is shown as the prediction. No internet or server is used after the page loads.
